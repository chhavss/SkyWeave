Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your next journey with SKYWEAVE.

Q. What is SKYWEAVE?

SKYWEAVE is an airline route planning platform that helps travelers find the best flight connections between airports. Whether you want the shortest route, the most affordable option, or the fastest journey, SKYWEAVE makes it easy to compare and choose.

Q. How do I search for flights?

Simply head to our Search page, enter your Source Airport, Destination Airport, and choose what you'd like to optimize for — shortest distance, lowest cost, or fastest duration. Hit "Find Route" and we'll instantly show you the best path available.

Q. Can I compare different routes?

Yes. SKYWEAVE lets you run multiple searches and compare routes based on distance, travel time, and total cost. Use the three optimization modes to see how your route changes depending on your priority.

Q. Is SKYWEAVE free to use?

Absolutely. SKYWEAVE is completely free to use. There are no hidden charges, subscription fees, or premium tiers. All route planning, airport information, and flight data are accessible to every visitor at no cost.

Q. Does SKYWEAVE support international airports?

Yes. Our network includes both domestic and international airports spanning multiple countries and continents. You can explore connections to major hubs and regional airports around the world directly from our platform.

Q. How can I contact support?

We're always happy to hear from you. Visit our Contact page to send us a message and a member of our team will get back to you as soon as possible.
